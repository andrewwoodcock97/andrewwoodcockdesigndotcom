MOTYF

Motyf is an International Symposium and Media Art Exhibition about the current and future forms of interactive and motion typography expressed through art, design, space and technology. For MOTYF 2018 I put forward an idea that has a futuristic approach. The project was called ‘The Virtual Frontier’. I created a series of images and typface that would create a strong identity.
