Maldives is an abstract adaptation that takes the viewer through a series of dream like states that represents the main character Elton. The short film is the cousin to silent films, there is no dialogue except for mumbles. My idea for the film titles is based off of this dream like state. With the use of hand rendered typography I have allowed the viewer to almost travel in and out of a dream like state without being too obvious. The hand rendered type is used as a representation of ‘natural’ and I chose this process to refer back to the natural system of a dream.
