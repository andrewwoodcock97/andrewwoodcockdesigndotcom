Habfat Hats

Lawriii Craic

Habfat Hats is an up and coming unisex streetwear clothing range. They supply clothes for both male and female. The products range from a variety of tracksuits and hats. I collaborated with Habfat Hats to create a series of photographs and videos that would be used on the social media platforms of the brand as well as the website designed and updated by David Pakenham and myself. For the proposed images, I wanted to bring out the colour and details of the product. The idea for the video was different as I got to bring in an urban styled street theme.
