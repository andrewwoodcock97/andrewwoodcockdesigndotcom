PUNK

Project

Designer

Catalogue Design

Andrew Woodcock

This project is about the evolution of the Punk attitude. Punk was considered a dead movement after its rise and fall in the 80s/90s but only through music and style. This project was constructed to find out how the Punk attitude is showing itself in new guises in the underground music scene of Hip-Hop, Raves, and Grime, with the use of homemade music, videos, clothes, and artwork. These are my own interpretations.
